AIR TO MOVE YOU FORWARDS
The Nike Air VaporMax R Men’s Running Shoe brings the reinvented cushioning system with an updated upper. A mesh design sits atop the lightweight, bouncy cushioning for the feeling that you’re defying gravity.
Lightweight Cushioning
Air cushioning provides maximum impact protection from heel to toe. Carved-out sections in the sole help keep the shoe incredibly flexible and lightweight.
Breathability
Made to let heat escape and air flow in, the mesh upper helps keep your feet cool. It’s lightweight and smooth for total comfort from start to finish.
Support
Lightweight overlays from the midfoot to the heel provide support without bulk. The midfoot overlays are synthetic leather with reflectivity to help you stand out, combining the look of a sneaker with the innovation of a performance shoe.
